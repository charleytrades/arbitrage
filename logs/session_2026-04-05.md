## 5. Strategy Tuning & Paper Trading

### Problem: No signals firing
The hardened strategy was too strict for Sunday low-volatility:
1. **Window too narrow**: 15-45s window meant markets spent most of their life outside evaluation range.
2. **Threshold too high**: 0.15% not met when market moves were 0.02-0.05%.
3. **Bybit confirmation required**: Silently blocked signals when Bybit disagreed or had no data.

### Changes Made
- Made Bybit confirmation **optional** (non-blocking) in `momentum_latency.py`.
- Added `.env` overrides: `MOMENTUM_WINDOW_START_SEC`, `MOMENTUM_WINDOW_END_SEC`, `VOLUME_CONFIRM_MULTIPLIER`.

### Demo run (0.005% threshold)
- Dropped threshold to `0.00005` to prove pipeline works.
- Signals fired within seconds — ETH, SOL, BTC paper trades executed.
- Telegram alerts delivered, dashboard updated, P&L tracked.
- Full pipeline confirmed working end-to-end.

### Final overnight settings (middle ground)
| Setting | Value | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| `MOMENTUM_THRESHOLD` | 0.001 (0.10%) | Between production 0.35% and demo 0.005% |
| `MOMENTUM_WINDOW_START_SEC` | 10 | Slightly earlier than production 15s |
| `MOMENTUM_WINDOW_END_SEC` | 180 | 3 minutes vs production 45s |
| `VOLUME_CONFIRM_MULTIPLIER` | 1.0 | Any volume counts |
| `MIN_SPREAD_PROFIT` | 0.003 | Slightly lower than production 0.005 |
| Bybit confirmation | Optional | Won't block signals |
